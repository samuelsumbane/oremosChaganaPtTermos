Privacy Policy - Oremos Changana-PT

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the Oremos Changana-PT application handles information when you use the application.

Oremos Changana-PT is developed and maintained by Samuel Sumbane and is available through the Google Play Store.

Information Collection and Use

Oremos Changana-PT does not require user registration and does not directly collect personally identifiable information from users.

Most of the application's functionality works entirely with data stored locally on the user's device.

Permissions Used

The application may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: Used to send reminders and important notifications related to the application.
  • File Access / Storage: Used to create and restore local backups selected by the user.

Advertising

Oremos Changana-PT may display rewarded advertisements through Google AdMob.

Advertisements are shown only when the user voluntarily chooses to watch an advertisement in exchange for access to specific features or content.

Google AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Diagnostic information
  • Performance and usage data

This information is processed by Google according to its own privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

However, third-party services integrated into the application, such as Google AdMob, may process certain technical information as required for their services.

Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect the information and data stored by the application. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

Children's Privacy
